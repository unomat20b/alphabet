# Goldilocks and the Three Bears

Once upon a time, there were three bears who lived together in a small house in the forest. There was a Great Big Bear, a Middle-sized Bear, and a Little Small Bear.

One morning, they made some porridge for breakfast. But it was too hot to eat, so they decided to go for a walk in the forest while it cooled down.

While they were away, a little girl named Goldilocks came to the house. She knocked on the door, but no one answered.

So she opened the door and went inside.

On the table, she saw three bowls of porridge.

"I’m hungry," she said.

First, she tasted the porridge of the Great Big Bear.
"This porridge is too hot!"

Then she tasted the porridge of the Middle-sized Bear.
"This porridge is too cold!"

Then she tasted the porridge of the Little Small Bear.
"This porridge is just right!"

And she ate it all up.

Next, Goldilocks saw three chairs.

She sat in the chair of the Great Big Bear.
"This chair is too hard!"

Then she sat in the chair of the Middle-sized Bear.
"This chair is too soft!"

Then she sat in the chair of the Little Small Bear.
"This chair is just right!"

But suddenly — crack! — the chair broke under her.

Goldilocks felt tired, so she went upstairs.

There she found three beds.

She lay down on the bed of the Great Big Bear.
"This bed is too hard!"

Then she lay on the bed of the Middle-sized Bear.
"This bed is too soft!"

Then she lay on the bed of the Little Small Bear.
"This bed is just right!"

And she fell fast asleep.

Soon, the three bears came home.

"Someone has been eating my porridge!" said the Great Big Bear.

"Someone has been eating my porridge!" said the Middle-sized Bear.

"Someone has been eating my porridge — and ate it all up!" cried the Little Small Bear.

Then they went to the living room.

"Someone has been sitting in my chair!" said the Great Big Bear.

"Someone has been sitting in my chair!" said the Middle-sized Bear.

"Someone has been sitting in my chair — and broken it!" cried the Little Small Bear.

Then they went upstairs.

"Someone has been sleeping in my bed!" said the Great Big Bear.

"Someone has been sleeping in my bed!" said the Middle-sized Bear.

"Someone has been sleeping in my bed — and she is still here!" cried the Little Small Bear.

Goldilocks woke up and saw the three bears.

She jumped out of bed, ran down the stairs, out of the house, and into the forest.

And she never came back again.
